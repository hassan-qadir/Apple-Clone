Blood Oxygen

A breath of
fresh innovation.

Breakthrough sensor. Powerful insights.The blood oxygen sensor is made up of four LED clusters and four photodiodes.Incorporated into the completely redesigned back crystal,the sensor works in concert with the Blood Oxygen app to determine your blood oxygen level.
Deeply illuminating.Green, red, and infrared LEDs shine
light onto the blood vessels in your wrist,and photodiodes
measure the amount of light reflected back.Advanced
algorithms then calculate the color of your blood,which indicates the amount
of oxygen present.

ECG

On your wrist.

Anytime.

Anywhere.

With the ECG app, Apple Watch Series 6 is capable of generating an ECG similar to a single-lead electrocardiogram.2 It’s a momentous achievement for a wearable device that can provide critical data for doctors and peace of mind for you.
