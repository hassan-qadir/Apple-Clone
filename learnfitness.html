A different kind of fitness experience is here. One that dynamically integrates your personal metrics from Apple Watch, along with music from your favorite artists, to inspire like no other workout in the world.

Metrics

Leave it all on the screen.

Your Apple Watch is equipped with sensors that, combined with advanced algorithms, give you all the metrics you need to track your workout.
With Fitness+, that information is synced to your iPhone, iPad, or Apple TV in real time to keep you motivated and engaged.
Put your heart into it.Literally. What’s your heart rate? Is your Exercise ring about to close? The answers are right there, so you’ll always know exactly how you’re doing without looking down at your wrist. Eyes forward. Progress follows.
A little healthy competition.For certain cardio workouts, the Burn Bar is there for an added push if you want it. It shows how you stack up against everyone in that particular workout. That’s even more go for your goals.
Workouts

An exercise in simplicity.

Apple Fitness+ makes it quick and easy for everyone to get the best workout on iPhone, iPad, or Apple TV. There are even workouts designed for different seasons of your life. A few taps let you put your body through exactly what you have in mind.
